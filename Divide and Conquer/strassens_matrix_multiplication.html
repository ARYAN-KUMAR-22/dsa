Common Mistakes

          

            
Wrong quadrant formulas
If even one M1 to M7 formula is off by a sign or quadrant reference, the final quadrants are incorrect.

            
Ignoring padding
Real implementations often pad odd-sized matrices to powers of two before applying the clean recursive split.

            
Constant-factor trap
Strassen is asymptotically faster, but for small matrices the extra additions and memory traffic can still lose in practice.

            
Combine confusion
Remember that C11, C12, C21, and C22 are built from different signed combinations of the seven products.
